About the Founder
16 Years of Export Excellence

Founded by Makhsudov Ozodbek Bakhromalievich (born August 21, 1983), NUR Group traces its roots to 2010 when Ozodbek began exporting Uzbek agricultural products as a sole proprietor.

Recognizing the growing demand and expanding international partnerships, he formally established "Tinchlik Meva Export" JV in February 2020. Primary trade corridors include the Russian Federation and Mongolia, with European market expansion underway.
